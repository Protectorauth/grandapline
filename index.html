Aoraki Mackenzie International Dark Sky Reserve

New Zealand's Finest Dark Sky

Mount Cook Village sits within one of only a handful of Gold-Tier International Dark Sky Reserves in the world. With virtually no light pollution, the Milky Way stretches from horizon to horizon — a spectacle best enjoyed from our hot pools or guided stargazing tours.
